About Us

ArtNauka was founded in 2010, inspired by a growing demand for intelligent, engaging entertainment. From our beginnings in Science & Art and interactive workshops, we pioneered game-based learning experiences for adults—blending education, creativity, and play.

Over the years, we’ve partnered with renowned companies and built a strong presence on the international stage. Our work has been featured by media outlets in 12 languages, and our journey is just getting started.

Where You’ve Seen Us

CryoEffect is perfect for conferences, exhibitions, and corporate events. We’ve amazed audiences at major international festivals and world-class venues, including:

  • Wanda Group (with a performance at the iconic Yellow Crane Tower)
  • Taobao Market Festival in Shanghai
  • Abu Dhabi Science Festival
  • Edinburgh International Science Festival
  • And many more across the globe
